The Movie Genuis: Ryan Fairbanks

Works

SpongeBob Meets World

This live action rendition of a cartoon classic struck our hearts with a riveting tale of friendsip and loss. Ryan Fairbanks made a great decision bringing the cartoon to real life giving the movie a personal feel. This film is a true masterpiece.

Chicken Little

This artistic film follows a chicken living in a "utopian" society blocked off from the rest of the world. The Chicken breaks free from the society only to be shocked by the harsh reality of the real world. Soon after his escape, the chicken gets shot by a group of hunters. There are many theories reguarding the message of this movie. Ryan Fairbanks says this is exactly what he wanted from this film; to make people think and craft their own theories.

The Matrix

Ryan Fairbanks took a risk rebooting a series that left fans with a bad taste in their mouths. Yet, because of the perfect execution, the film came off as authentic and reminded us of the first Matrix that we know and love.

Awards

Fairbanks has been nominated for 8 academy awards and won 4. 2 for best director and 2 for best picture.
